Tres Leches

Description

Tres leches is a classic Latin American dessert made from a light sponge or butter cake that is soaked in a mixture of three milks: evaporated milk, sweetened condensed milk, and heavy cream (or whole milk). This soaking gives the cake a rich, moist texture while still keeping it airy and tender. The name “tres leches” literally means “three milks,” referring to the signature blend that sets it apart from other cakes.

After the cake absorbs the milk mixture, it is usually topped with a layer of lightly sweetened whipped cream and sometimes garnished with cinnamon, fruit, or toasted coconut. The result is a decadent yet refreshing dessert, celebrated for its balance of sweetness and creaminess. Tres leches is popular across Mexico, Central America, the Caribbean, and beyond, often served at birthdays, holidays, and festive gatherings.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Sponge Cake base
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Whole milk
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Evaporated milk
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Cinnamon
  • Strawberries

Steps

  1. Bake The Cake. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat egg yolks with ¾ cup sugar until pale and thick. Stir in milk and vanilla.
  4. In another bowl, beat egg whites until soft peaks form. Gradually add ¼ cup sugar and beat until stiff peaks form.
  5. Gently fold yolk mixture into the flour mixture, then fold in egg whites until just combined.
  6. Pour into prepared dish and bake 25 to 30 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool completely.
  7. Soak the cake. In a bowl, whisk evaporated milk, sweetened condensed milk, and heavy cream.
  8. Poke holes all over the cooled cake with a fork. Slowly pour milk mixture evenly over the cake. Refrigerate at least 4 hours or overnight to absorb.
  9. Make the topping. Beat whipping cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla until stiff peaks form.
  10. Spread over chilled cake. Garnish with cinnamon or fruit if desired.
